Game Rules:

1. Each player starts with one general (your most important tile) and a few troops on the board.
2. Command your army to move by (w,a,s,d) and (mouse-click) and capture other tiles.
3. To capture a tile, the tile you're moving from must have more troops than the tile you're moving to.
4. Each tile you occupy increases your army by 1 every turn.
5. Cities and captured generals become part of your territory and produce extra troops for you every turn.
6. You can only see the tiles that your army has visited.
7. To win the game, you need to find and capture your opponents' generals and occupy as much of the tiles as possible.
